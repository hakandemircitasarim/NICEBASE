### Description (English)

```
NICEBASE - Your Personal Emotional Anchor

Preserve and cherish your beautiful memories. NICEBASE helps you maintain your emotional well-being by storing and reminding you of happy moments in your life.

KEY FEATURES:

📦 Memory Vault
Store your precious memories with photos, text, categories, and emotional intensity levels. Organize them by life areas and create meaningful connections.

🎲 Recall Widget
When you need a boost, let NICEBASE show you random beautiful memories from your collection.

💝 Relationship Saver
Create personalized memory slideshows for special people in your life. Strengthen your relationships by sharing meaningful moments.

🤖 Aiya AI
Get personalized emotional support through our AI companion. Aiya helps you reflect, understand your emotions, and find positive perspectives.

📊 Statistics & Analytics
Track your emotional journey with detailed statistics, trends, and insights about your memories.

✨ Features:
• Offline support - Access your memories anytime
• Cloud sync - Your data is safely backed up
• Dark mode - Comfortable viewing in any lighting
• Multiple languages - Turkish and English
• Daily reminders - Never forget to reflect
• Streak system - Build positive habits
• Achievements & badges - Gamified experience
• Export options - Download your memories (JSON, PDF, CSV)
• Memory templates - Quick prompts to get started
• Search & filter - Find memories easily

PRIVACY & SECURITY:
Your memories are private and encrypted. We use secure cloud storage and never share your data with third parties.

Download NICEBASE today and start building your emotional resilience!
```
